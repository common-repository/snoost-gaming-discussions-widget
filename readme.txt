A gaming forum widget which lists the latest gaming discussions and threads from the Snoost community.

== Description ==

List the latest gaming discussions in a simple sidebar on your blog, with the most recent activity, threads and forum posts from the official Snoost gaming community. Select the exact number of posts to show and whether or not to include a “Create new discussion” button at the bottom of the widget.

= An official gaming forum =

Participate in discussions about your favourite game, or let your visitors do the same, and let your gaming blog come to life with interactive threads and activity about gaming related subjects right in your sidebar or footer. Set the title, to personalise the widget for your audience.

== Installation ==

= From your WordPress dashboard =

1. Go to 'Plugins' > 'Add New'
2. Search for 'Snoost: Gaming Discussions Widget'
3. Activate 'Snoost: Gaming Discussions Widget' from your plugins page

= From WordPress.org =

1. Download 'Snoost: Gaming Discussions Widget' plugin here
2. Use an FTP client to upload the 'snoost-gaming-discussions-widget' folder to your '/wp-content/plugins/' directory, or simply go to 'Plugins' > 'Add New' > 'Upload plugin' from your WordPress dashboard and upload the .zip file there
3. Activate 'Snoost: Gaming Discussions Widget' from the plugins page

After activating the plugin, all you have to do is go to ‘Appearance’ > ‘Widgets’ and drag the new widget into a sidebar or footer of your choosing. Then fill out the desired information, and you are done.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How to change number of posts? =

Simply edit the post count in the widget formular from the admin panel in WordPress.

= How ofte are posts updated? =

About every 10 minutes.
